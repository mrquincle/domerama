Welcome to Domerama

Nov 232011
 

We're from the Ottawa-Montreal area

Domerama exists to help understand and build geodesic domes and other types of dome-shaped structures.

For the DIY people out there: building your own geodesic dome is an ideal DIY project that anyone at any age and skill level can contribute to. You can start with a simple geodesic dome with materials found at your local hardware store. If you have basic equipment and tools, you can make it at home. We have several geodesic dome calculators and information on many types of similar structures.

For the non-DIY crowd: on the other hand, purchasing a dome kit may be just right for you; the more complex and bigger you want your dome, the more attentive to details you need to be. Domerama helps you understand what to look for so you can make an informed decision.

Whatever is right for you, Domerama is here to give you knowledge so as to take informed decisions. This is why the geodesic dome structures on this site give more than just strut lengths; we also give you estimates on how big your dome cover will be, cost of materials and even seating capacity!
